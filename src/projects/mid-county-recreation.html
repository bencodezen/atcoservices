MID-COUNTY RECREATION COMMUNITY CENTER

BASIC INFORMATION

Location: Silver Spring, MD

Description: A Mechanical & Plumbing LEED project that involved new construction of a one-story recreation community center, complete with arts & crafts rooms, exercise rooms, gymnasium, kitchens, offices, etc. The Mechanical package included: 3 Energy Recovery Units (ERU), 14 Geothermal Water Source Heat Pumps, VAV's, 7 Fans, 2 Pumps, 1 Expansion Tank, 3 Kiln Hoods, 1 Kitchen Hood, Ductwork, Piping, and DDC Controls.
