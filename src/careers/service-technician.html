Service Technician

Responsibilities:

  • Read and interpret wiring diagrams.
  • Troubleshoot, repair, and replace existing and new HVAC systems.
  • Interact with clients in a professional and courteous manner.
  • Work alongside colleagues in an efficient and professional manner.
  • Correspond with office (via phone) personnel regarding job status.
  • Properly document job progress.

Requirements:

  • Minimum of 5 years HVAC work experience
  • CFC License
  • Organization & Computer Skills
  • Necessary Work Tools
  • Clean Driving Record
  • NATE Certification a plus
  • Apprentice/Journeymany License a plus
