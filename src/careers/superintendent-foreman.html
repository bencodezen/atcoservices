Superintendent / Foreman

 

Responsibilities:

  • Successfully work and coordinate all jobsite activities across all projects.
  • Interact with clients in a professional and courteous manner.
  • Account for all men and working activity during each work day.
  • Attend weekly progress meetings (as necessary per project).
  • Read and interpret plans and specifications
  • Correspond with office (via email and/or phone) personnel regarding job progress, material ordering, and employees. A site foreman must also report to the office at a minimum of once a week to provide documentation and work progress.
  • Knowledge & ability to install residential and light commercial equipment and associated piping and ductwork.

Requirements:

  • Minimum of 7 years HVAC work experience.
  • Organizational & Computer Skills
  • Clean Driving Record
  • Proper grooming and work attire
  • Necessary work tools
